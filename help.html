Help Section

Add Task

To add a task to the backlog, you have to give it a Title and Description. Also you have to add a Category, Due Date and Urgency. Finaly alteast one employee has to be assigned.

Backlog

In the Backlog the created tasks are shown. The Category, Details and the Assignment is show here on one quick look.
Before moving to the Taskboard you are able to delete the task again if needed, by clicking on the Trashcan.
To move the task to the Taskboard you have to click on the arrow.

Board

Inside the Taskboard you are able to move the tasks in different categories. "To-do", "In Progress", "Testing" and "Done". With "drag and drop" the tasks are easily moved to other categories.
